What is Forjit AI? Free AI Tools Platform for India — Explained

Published April 2025 · 5 min read · forjitai.in

If you've come across the name Forjit AI or the website forjitai.in and want to know what it is — this guide explains everything clearly.

Quick Answer: Forjit AI is a free online platform with two main offerings — an AI content generator (create apps, plans, documents from a prompt) and 41+ free calculators built specifically for Indian users.

What Does Forjit AI Do?

Forjit AI (forjitai.in) is a free AI platform made in India. It has two separate sections:

1. AI Content Generator

The main app at forjitai.in is an AI-powered generator. You type a prompt in plain English and get:

  • Web Apps — complete single-file HTML apps that run in any browser
  • Mobile Apps — PWA-optimised apps for phone screens
  • Meal Plans — weekly/monthly plans with calories, prep time
  • Workout Plans — exercises with sets, reps, rest times
  • Resumes — downloadable as PDF or DOCX
  • Presentations — downloadable as PPTX
  • Business Reports — formatted PDFs
  • Daily Diary Apps — with mood tracking and localStorage

2. Free Online Calculators (41+ Tools)

The tools section at forjitai.in/tools has 41+ free calculators — no signup, no ads, no API key needed:

🏦 EMI Calculator 🧾 GST Calculator 📈 SIP Calculator 📋 Income Tax 💼 Salary / CTC 🛡️ PPF Calculator ⚖️ BMI Calculator 📝 Word Counter 📱 QR Generator 🔐 Password Gen

Is Forjit AI Really Free?

Yes. Here's the breakdown:

  • All 41+ calculators — completely free, no account needed
  • AI generator — free if you bring your own API key from Groq (generous free tier) or OpenRouter
  • No hidden fees — no subscription, no paywall on tools
Tip: Groq gives free API access to Llama 3.3 70B with generous rate limits — enough for casual use of Forjit AI's generator at no cost.

What Makes Forjit AI Different?

There are many AI tools and calculator sites. Here's what sets Forjit AI apart:

  1. Built for India — Indian tax slabs (FY 2025-26), GST, PPF, HRA, CGPA calculations — not generic Western tools
  2. AI + Tools in one place — rare combination of AI generator + calculators on one platform
  3. Privacy-first — all calculations run in your browser, no data sent to servers
  4. Unique features — like EMI prepayment impact, goal-based SIP reverse calculator, tax saving suggestions
  5. No fluff — clean, fast, mobile-friendly design

How to Use Forjit AI

Using the Calculator Tools

  1. Go to forjitai.in/tools
  2. Pick any tool — EMI, GST, Income Tax, etc.
  3. Enter your values
  4. Get instant results — no signup, no loading

Using the AI Generator

  1. Go to forjitai.in
  2. Click Settings → add your Groq or OpenRouter API key (free to get)
  3. Choose your tab: Create App, Content Planner, or Documents
  4. Type your prompt and click Generate
  5. Download, preview or iterate on the result

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Forjit AI safe to use?

Yes. The calculator tools send zero data anywhere — they're pure JavaScript running locally in your browser. For the AI generator, your API key is stored only in your device's IndexedDB (browser storage), never transmitted to Forjit AI's servers.

Does Forjit AI have an app?

Forjit AI is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On Android/iOS, you can "Add to Home Screen" from your browser to use it like a native app. A dedicated Android app is in the roadmap.

Who built Forjit AI?

Forjit AI was built by a solo developer based in Karnataka, India. It's an independent project — not backed by any company or VC funding.

Is Forjit AI available in Hindi or other Indian languages?

The interface is currently in English. Support for Hindi, Kannada, Tamil and other Indian languages is planned for future updates.

How does Forjit AI compare to ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI chatbot. Forjit AI is a specialised tool — it generates structured outputs (apps, planners, documents) and has dedicated India-specific calculators. They serve different purposes. Forjit AI uses open-source models (free) while ChatGPT uses GPT-4 (paid).
