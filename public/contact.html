🔐 Privacy & Data Requests

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access — know what data we hold (email if you signed in, GA analytics aggregate)
  • Correction — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent — opt out of Google Analytics at any time

To delete your local data immediately (no email needed):
Browser Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Site data for forjitai.in
This removes all IndexedDB data including your email, API key memory, and calculator history.

For formal data requests, email forjitai@gmail.com with subject line: "Data Request — [Your Name]"
