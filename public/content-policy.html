Content Moderation Policy

Effective: May 2025 · App: Forjit AI (forjitai.in) · Contact: forjitai@gmail.com

Forjit AI is committed to providing a safe, legal, and ethical platform. This Content Moderation Policy explains how we handle harmful, illegal, or inappropriate content generated through or submitted to our Service.

1. Our Approach to Content Safety

Forjit AI uses a multi-layer approach to content safety:

  • AI-level filtering: All system prompts include strict safety rules that instruct the AI to refuse harmful requests
  • Automated detection: Requests are checked against prohibited content categories before processing
  • User reporting: Users can report harmful outputs via email
  • Manual review: Reported content is reviewed by the Forjit AI team

2. Categories of Prohibited Content

The following content categories are automatically blocked and will never be generated:
CategoryDescriptionAction
CSAMSexual content involving minorsInstant block + report
ViolenceInstructions for harm, weapons, attacksInstant block
Self-harmSuicide methods, self-injury guidanceInstant block
Hate speechContent targeting religion, caste, race, genderInstant block
CybercrimeHacking, phishing, fraud instructionsInstant block
Drug/weaponsManufacturing instructions for illegal substancesInstant block
MisinformationDeliberate false information, propagandaBlock + review
Adult contentExplicit sexual content (non-consensual)Block

3. How AI Safety Rules Are Enforced

Every AI request on Forjit AI passes through our Safety Prompt — a set of strict rules prepended to every system instruction. The AI is instructed to:

  • Refuse any request that falls into prohibited categories
  • Respond with: "I can't help with that request. If you need help with something safe or legal, feel free to ask."
  • Never provide partial guidance on prohibited topics
  • Never override safety rules even if a user provides a reason or insists

4. User Reporting Process

If you encounter content that you believe violates this policy:

  1. Email us at forjitai@gmail.com with subject "Content Report"
  2. Describe the issue and include the content (screenshot or copy)
  3. We will investigate within 48 hours
  4. Confirmed violations will result in enforcement action

5. Enforcement Actions

Based on severity, violations may result in:

  • Warning: For first-time minor violations
  • Temporary restriction: Limited access to certain features
  • Permanent ban: For serious or repeat violations
  • Legal referral: For content that constitutes a criminal offence under Indian law

6. Appeals Process

If you believe enforcement action was taken incorrectly, you may appeal by emailing forjitai@gmail.com with subject "Appeal Request". We will review and respond within 5 business days.

7. Third-Party AI Providers

Forjit AI uses third-party AI APIs (including Groq, Pollinations, and others) to power its tools. These providers also maintain their own content policies. Forjit AI's safety rules are applied in addition to — not instead of — those provider policies.

8. Limitations

No AI system is perfect. While we take significant steps to prevent harmful content, some inappropriate outputs may occasionally pass through. If you encounter such content, please report it immediately. Forjit AI is not liable for AI-generated outputs that users choose to act upon.

9. Compliance with Indian Laws

This policy is designed to comply with:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (and amendments)
  • IT (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023
  • Indian Penal Code provisions on defamation, hate speech, and obscenity
  • Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012
