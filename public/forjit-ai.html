Forjit AI

Forjit AI (forjitai.in) is a free Indian AI tools platform made in India. Forjit AI provides free online calculators, an Indian cooking planner, and an AI content generator — all completely free with no signup required.

Forjit AI is designed specifically for Indian users, with tools covering Indian income tax (FY 2025-26), GST calculations, EMI calculations, SIP returns, and 72 authentic Indian recipes from North, South, East and West India.

What is Forjit AI?

Forjit AI is an AI-powered web application available at www.forjitai.in. The name Forjit AI combines "forge" (to build) with "it" and "AI" — representing the mission to help Indians build and create using artificial intelligence tools.

Forjit AI uses large language models including Llama, Qwen and DeepSeek via Groq and OpenRouter APIs. Users bring their own API keys — Forjit AI never stores or transmits user API keys to any server.
