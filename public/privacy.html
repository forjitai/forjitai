5. Legal Basis (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, we process personal data under:

  • Consent — for optional email login and Google Analytics cookies (given via consent banner)
  • Legitimate interest — for functional localStorage (tool value memory) which directly serves the user

6. Your Rights

Under the DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access — know what data we hold about you (we hold very little)
  • Correction — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for analytics at any time
  • Nomination — nominate someone to exercise your rights

To exercise any right: email forjitai@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

To delete your local data immediately: open browser settings → Clear site data for forjitai.in.

11. Security

  • All traffic is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • No passwords — email-only optional login prevents password database risks
  • API keys stored in IndexedDB on your device, never transmitted to our servers
  • All calculator computations run client-side — no data in transit
  • We do not store payment information (no payment processing on this site currently)
If you discover a security vulnerability, please email forjitai@gmail.com responsibly before public disclosure.
