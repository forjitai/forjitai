💡 Nutrition Tips

  • 1 kg of fat ≈ 7,700 calories — a 500 cal/day deficit = ~0.5 kg/week loss
  • Protein helps preserve muscle while cutting — aim for 1.6–2.2g per kg of bodyweight
  • Track meals with apps like MyFitnessPal or HealthifyMe
  • TDEE varies ±15% based on genetics, NEAT, hormones
  • Don't go below 1,200 cal/day (women) or 1,500 cal/day (men) without medical advice
