💡 FD Tips

  • Interest from FD is fully taxable at your income tax slab rate
  • TDS @ 10% is deducted if annual interest exceeds ₹40,000 (₹50,000 for senior citizens)
  • Submit Form 15G/15H to avoid TDS if income is below taxable limit
  • Laddering FDs (1yr, 2yr, 3yr) gives liquidity + better average rates
  • Senior citizens get 0.25–0.50% extra rate at most banks
