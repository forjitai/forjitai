💡 About Ideal Weight

  • No single formula works for everyone — muscle mass, age, and body type matter
  • BMI 18.5–22.9 is recommended for Asians (slightly lower than Western standards)
  • Athletes often weigh more than "ideal" due to muscle mass
  • Focus on body fat percentage rather than just weight
  • Consult a doctor for personalized healthy weight goals
