💡 Beating Inflation in India

  • FD (~7%) barely beats 6% inflation — real return is ~1%
  • Equity mutual funds historically returned 12–15% CAGR — real return 6–9%
  • Gold averages ~10% in India over long periods
  • Real estate: highly location-dependent, 8–12% typically
  • Avoid keeping excess cash in savings account (3.5–4% return)
