💡 RD vs FD — When to choose?

  • RD: When you want to save monthly from salary — builds discipline
  • FD: When you have a lump sum to invest at once
  • RD interest is taxable — TDS applies if annual interest exceeds ₹40,000
  • Post Office RD: 6.7% p.a. (Q1 2024-25), government-backed safety
  • Premature withdrawal allowed after 3 months (with penalty)
