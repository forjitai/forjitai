💡 Retirement Planning Tips

  • Start early — investing ₹5K/month from age 25 vs 35 creates a massive difference
  • Rule of 25: You need 25× your annual expenses as corpus (4% withdrawal rule)
  • EPF + PPF + NPS form a strong debt foundation for retirement
  • Diversify: equity for growth, debt for stability, gold as hedge
  • Don't ignore healthcare costs — plan for ₹50-100L for medical expenses
  • Inflation is the biggest retirement risk — equity must be part of your portfolio
