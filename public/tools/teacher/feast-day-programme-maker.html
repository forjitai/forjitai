What is Feast Day & Parish Day Programme Maker?

Feast Day & Parish Day Programme Maker is a free AI-powered tool by Forjit AI designed for Indian teachers and educators. It helps you plan complete parish feast day celebrations with timetable, mass, cultural programme, and announcements. Works on mobile and desktop. No signup required. CBSE, ICSE, and State Board friendly.

Yes. It plans for all sizes from small chapel feasts to large parish festivals.

Yes. Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, and Hindi are all supported.
