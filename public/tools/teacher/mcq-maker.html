What is MCQ Question Paper Maker?

MCQ Question Paper Maker is a free AI-powered tool by Forjit AI designed for Indian teachers and educators. It helps you auto-generate multiple choice questions with answers for any subject and topic. Works on mobile and desktop. No signup required. CBSE, ICSE, and State Board friendly.

Yes. Review and edit as needed before use in exams or practice tests.
