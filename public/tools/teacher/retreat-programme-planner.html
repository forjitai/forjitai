What is Retreat Programme Planner?

Retreat Programme Planner is a free AI-powered tool by Forjit AI designed for Indian teachers and educators. It helps you plan a complete spiritual retreat programme with schedule, talks, prayer sessions, and group activities. Works on mobile and desktop. No signup required. CBSE, ICSE, and State Board friendly.
