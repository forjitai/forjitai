Frequently Asked Questions

What is a grading rubric?
A rubric is a scoring guide with criteria and performance levels to evaluate student work consistently.
Can I create rubrics for projects?
Yes. Works for science projects, essays, presentations, lab reports, art work, and more.
Can I set custom criteria?
Yes. Enter your own criteria in the 'Key Criteria' field or leave blank for AI to suggest.
Is the total marks flexible?
Yes. Choose from 10, 20, 25, 50, or 100 marks.
Can I print the rubric?
Yes. Copy the output and paste into Word or Google Docs for printing.
